Hello! I’m a senior Mechanical Engineering major with minors in robotics and artificial intelligence (AI) at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology (RHIT).

I have been designing, programming and building solutions to everyday problems for 10 years. I developed my problem solving and teamwork skills building and programming robots for 12 years as a FIRST team member.

When I’m not studying, you’ll find me leading the RHIT MATE underwater robotics team where I indulge my passion of designing and improving compliant mechanisms and elecromechanical systems; enjoying the great outdoors on my mountain bike; or at the piano laying my heart bare in a musical composition.
